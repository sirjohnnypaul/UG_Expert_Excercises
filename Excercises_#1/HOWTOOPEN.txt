To open folder, find Visual Studio Code on your PC/Laptop and open dowloaded folder with the program. 
After that go to dependencies tab and search for Live Server, install it. After installation refresh 
the program. Choose index.html file and click it to display content. Then on the bottom bar of the programm click LIVE SERVER.
It will open the browser automatically. If chrome is not set as default, simply copy the url of opened page and paste it to chrome.
On chrome click right button of your mouse and then explore. Choose console on the tabs and here you are. Now results of your code 
should be visible. Enjoy!
